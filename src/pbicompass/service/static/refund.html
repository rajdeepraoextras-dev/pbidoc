Refund Policy

Effective 11 July 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for paid PBICompass subscriptions (Pro and Business). It applies alongside our Terms of Service.

1. Free plan

The Free plan is not billed, so no refund applies.

2. Cancelling a paid plan

You can cancel your Pro or Business subscription at any time from your account dashboard. When you cancel mid-cycle, we refund the unused, pro-rated portion of your current billing period — calculated from the day your cancellation takes effect to the end of the period you already paid for — back to your original payment method. You keep access to your plan's features for the remainder of the period you've already paid for, unless you request an immediate refund and cancellation instead.

3. Downgrading between paid plans

If you downgrade from Business to Pro mid-cycle, the price difference for the unused portion of the current period is refunded on the same pro-rated basis as a cancellation.

4. How refunds are processed

All payments and refunds are processed by our payment processor and merchant of record, Paddle.com Market Limited ("Paddle"). Refunds are issued to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card provider.

5. Requesting a refund

To cancel or request a refund, use your account dashboard, or email rajdeepraoextras@gmail.com with your account email and the billing period in question. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

6. Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us first — we can typically resolve refund requests faster than a card chargeback, and chargebacks may result in account suspension while the dispute is investigated.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
