Billing, answered

How does billing work?

Paid plans are billed monthly by Paddle.com Market Limited, our merchant of record. Your report quota resets at the start of each billing period; unused reports don't roll over.

What happens if I go over my monthly quota?

New generation jobs are declined with a clear message until your quota resets next month, or until you upgrade to a higher plan — whichever comes first.

Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Yes, from your account dashboard. Cancelling or downgrading mid-cycle refunds the unused, pro-rated portion of what you already paid — see our Refund Policy.

Do I need my own AI provider key?

No. AI-written documentation is included on every plan — there's no key to manage. Prefer full determinism or an air-gapped run? The offline engine produces complete documentation with no AI at all.
